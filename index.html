ABOUT JORDAN

The Legacy of Air Jordan

In 1984, Michael Jordan signed with Nike, creating what would become the most iconic basketball shoe line in history. The Air Jordan 1 was banned by the NBA for not meeting uniform standards, but Nike paid the fines and turned it into a marketing triumph.

Today, Jordan Brand is a subsidiary of Nike and continues to release new models and retro editions of classic styles. The brand has expanded beyond basketball to include lifestyle and training products.
